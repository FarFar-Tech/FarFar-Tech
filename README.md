## 🧔🏻 About Me

> **Postdoc researcher at UTU in Finland** | **Senior Remote Sensing Researcher** | **AI Research Enthusiast**

I am a dedicated **Remote Sensing Data Scientist** specializing in leveraging **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** to analyze **Earth Observation (EO)** data. I translate complex problems—from **land cover mapping** to **change detection**—into high-impact, automated solutions. My expertise spans the complete pipeline, from **pre-processing petabytes of satellite imagery** (GIS, Optical, SAR, LiDAR) to developing and deploying **deep learning models** for large-scale geospatial analysis.

### Core Strengths
- **EO ML Pipelines:** Full-stack development, from cloud-native data ingestion to model deployment.
- **Deep Learning for RS:** Expertise in Computer Vision models tailored for satellite and aerial imagery analysis.
- **Scalable Geospatial MLOps:** Building robust, automated systems for petabyte-scale data processing.
- **Spatiotemporal Analytics:** Advanced techniques for time-series data analysis and anomaly detection.
- **Research-to-Production:** Translating cutting-edge Remote Sensing research into real-world applications.
- **Generative AI Solution:** Creating RAG and AI Agents for EO applications.

## 🛰️​ Projects (Experience)
- **MaaTi (Apr 2021 - Apr 2022):** The project is implemented under the leadership of the Natural Resources Institute Finland in cooperation with the Geological Survey of Finland, the University of Helsinki, and the University of Turku. Information on soil properties is needed for decision-making in agriculture, forestry, and environmental management, in land-use and land-use policy planning, and in assessing greenhouse gas emissions. The soil data collected so far are partly unused in studying soil properties and processes. The aim of this project is to develop and test a set of methods that can quickly and cost-effectively produce soil data for the entire country. 👉🏽 [HERE](https://mmm.fi/en/-/maaperatiedon-kehittaminen-maati-) 
- **AI-ARC (Apr 2022 - Apr 2024):** The AI-ARC 'Artificial Intelligence based Virtual Control Room for the Arctic' project's main objective is to create an innovative and user-friendly AI based platform, the Virtual Control Room (VCR), that has the power to greatly improve maritime situational awareness, decision-making, communication, available rescue resources, and thus the safety of all maritime actors, particularly in the Arctic Sea. The AI-ARC project developed a shared collaboration workspace based on innovative and efficient AI services, a VCR that significantly enhances border and external security, and supports cooperation in managing external borders in the Arctic. 👉🏽 [HERE](https://ai-arc.eu/)
- **EIS (Mar 2024 - Oct 2025):** Exploration Information System (EIS) proposal has been compiled by a consortium, which consists of 17 partners from leading research institutes (4), academia (5), service providers (4), and industry (4). The consortium members come from six European Union member states (FI, FR, DE, ES, CZ, SE) and South Africa. EIS aims to raise awareness of the importance of critical raw materials to the EU’s transition, economy, and welfare by developing the "EIS Toolkit” and the “EIS QGIS Wizard”. These tools are open-source and provide critical information for the mining sector and geoscientists. 👉🏽 [HERE](https://eis-he.eu/)
- **AIMEX (Oct 2025 - March 2026):** The AIMEX project proposes to use machine learning to create mineral prospectivity maps that have direct application potential in the global mining industry. The consortium brings together research teams from the Geological Survey of Finland, University of Turku, and Finnish Environment Institute, providing a strong combination of expertise in mineral prospectivity mapping, machine learning, and sustainable development. The project leverages unsupervised and supervised machine learning methods across various stages of the prospectivity mapping process, with the aim of creating a decision-making system for mineral exploration that incorporates mineral resource assessments, economic factors, natural conservation areas, and other land-use constraints. 👉🏽 [HERE](https://www.gtk.fi/en/research-project/artificial-intelligence-in-mineral-exploration-aimex/)
- **USVA (Apr 2026 - Present):** The USVA (Uncrewed Surface Vessels for Automated Critical Infrastructure Protection) project develops unmanned surface vessel (USV)-based maritime surveillance solutions for the needs of private critical infrastructure owners. The project aims to improve the surveillance of ports, offshore energy cables, telecommunications networks, and wind turbines, especially in challenging weather conditions and during GPS or communication jamming. 👉🏽 [HERE](https://www.turkuamk.fi/en/project/usva-uncrewed-surface-vessels-for-automated-usva-maritime-critical-infrastructure-protection/)
